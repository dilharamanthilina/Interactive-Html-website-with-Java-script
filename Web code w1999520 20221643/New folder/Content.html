Football Clubs


A Football club or an association football club are organizations which perform sports activites against other football association clubs.

Thousands of Football clubs are found around the globe while also being different from one another.

Manchester City, Manchester United, Real Madrid, Barcelona, Liverpool are some of well known Football clubs.

Football clubs have been in practise since the 19th centuary and will continue to develop and spread in the future as well.

Professiotional Football players join these clubs to compete against other clubs.

Most of these clubs are sponsored by wealthy people or known branded goods to market themselves.

There is a very large fan base due to the high competitiveness and matches being held regularly.
